‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register a new member?

Navigate to "Register New" from the sidebar. Fill in the required personal information, address, and contact details. Upload a 2x2 photo and submit the form.

How to verify an ID card?

Go to "Verify ID" page. You can either scan the QR code using the built-in scanner or manually enter the ID number for verification.

How to renew an expired ID?

Find the member in the Member Directory, click the renew button (üîÑ), verify their information, and process the renewal fee.

How to print ID cards?

Go to "ID Cards" page, select the member's card, and click the Print button. You can also batch print multiple cards at once.
